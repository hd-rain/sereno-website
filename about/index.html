About Sereno

Our mission is hardwired into our DNA

Sereno was built with a clear vision: bring transparency to the insurance world and elevate parametric solutions to help businesses manage the financial impact of weather events across all industries.

As climate volatility increases, too many risks remain poorly covered, misunderstood, or excluded.

We believe insurance should be simple to understand, grounded in science, and designed to deliver predictable financial outcomes when it matters most.

That is what Sereno is built to do.

Our values are the cornerstone of our daily mission.

Innovation with purpose

We innovate to solve real insurance problems.

Our technology and parametric structures are designed to expand what can be insured, responsibly and at scale.

Scientific rigor

Data alone is not enough.

Our solutions are grounded in climate science, expert validation, and transparent methodologies to ensure accuracy, credibility, and long-term reliability.

Passionate experts

At the core of Sereno is a team of experienced meteorologists and insurance professionals.

From meteorology to underwriting, we commit to excellence in every solution we deliver.

End customer focused

We design everything with users in mind.

Clear contracts, understandable triggers, proactive alerts, and predictable payouts are not features. They are expectations.
